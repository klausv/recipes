Ingredients:
 - 1 rutabaga
 - handfull carrots
 
Directions:
 1. Peel rutabaga, cut in carrots and rutabaga in pieces of 2 x 3 x 1 cm approximately  
 
 2. Boil in water for 20 minutes
 3. Put in blender together with 1 dl of olive oil, 1-2 cut chilis, salt and pepper.  
 4. Run blender until everything is well stewed.
